Summer Jobs

Are you too old to be a camper? Do you have a passion for sharing your faith with others? Do you want to be part of an amazing team in the summer of 2023?  If so then please consider applying to be on our summer staff this year. Once you click apply now, create an account and it will have you complete the application and list your references with their email and contact information. Thanks for applying to join our staff!

2023 Summer Staff

Most of the staff that we hire are general summer staff that are cross trained in many areas. These staff get their lifeguard certification if possible, high ropes and low ropes certifications, food handler etc. They also learn how to be a counselor in the cabin, clean around camp and help in any other areas as needed. These staff change jobs from week to week or throughout their day if needed. Being flexible and willing to learn is key.

*We are hiring those 18 and older at this time. We have increased the wages and food and housing is included

*Staff training starts June 9th and runs for 2 weeks and you will work between 8 and 10 weeks this summer

*You will need to have completed your mandated reporter training and have copies of your child abuse, criminal history and FBI fingerprinting (if you have lived outside of PA in the last 10 years) completed before starting work. (volunteer clearances are not accepted for employees) Clearances are only good for 5 years or until you change your address.

* All staff will be hired as “summer staff” unless in a special position members will be trained in the kitchen, crafts, counseling, adventure and lifeguarding. You will be assigned a specific job for the week but your assignment may change weekly as needed to due camp numbers

Jobs 2023 Open positions

Kitchen Manager
Our Kitchen manager oversees the kitchen as a whole and runs a shift in the kitchen. They implement the menu, manage allergies and make sure the kitchen stays clean and organized.

Apply Here

Kitchen AM and PM leads
‍ Our kitchen leads make sure that the staff completes the necessary tasks to get meals out on time and keep the kitchen clean. They work underneath the kitchen manager and often our kitchen manager acts as one of these leads. Kitchen experience is beneficial but a strong leader can also be taught the needed kitchen skills.

Apply Here

Cleaning Crew
‍ We are in need of adults that have attention to detail and flexibility in the schedule. Most hours will be worked Friday afternoon and evening or Saturday morning. Some hours other days during the week are available. Those on the cleaning crew will make sure camp is cleaned at the end of the week and ready for the next set of campers coming in. The cleaning crew will clean the cabins and other areas around camp.

Apply Here

Lifeguard
‍ If you have your lifeguard certification or think that you can get it please consider applying. We have a great inground pool that is a favorite among the campers. We offer lifeguard training as part of our training week. Our lifeguards also work around camp in other areas when not on duty as a lifeguard.

Apply Here

Adventure Team
‍ Our adventure team members need to enjoy heights and be willing to step out of their comfort zone as they run the zipline, lead canoe trips, go rock climbing, belay and lead the low ropes course. Not all of our staff needs to do this but if it is a passion of yours or you are not scared of heights adding this certification would be great for you.
