Campground Retreats & Rentals

Host Your Own Retreat

Here at Wesley Woods we love hosting groups. Our accommodations, activities and meeting spaces are perfect for any group size. So whether you’re a family reunion, work retreat, youth group, or even a church group looking for a get-away, we’ve got you covered.
‍

We are located near the Allegheny National Forest and there are a lot of exciting things to do within an hour and a half of the sites.

  • Kinzua Dam (45 minutes)
  • Kinzua Bridge (1.5 hours)
  • Cook Forest (1 hour)
  • Presque Isle State Park (1.25 hours)
  • Peek’n Peak (50 minutes)
  • Drake Well Museum & Park (30 minutes)
  • Allegheny National Forest (20 minutes)

Lodging

We have lodging for groups from 4 to 200+. All of our lodges are 4-season lodges with heat, hot water, and in-lodge toilet and bathing facilities.

Food Service

Wesley Woods happily accommodates most dietary restrictions such as gluten free, vegetarian, vegan, lactose intolerance, and most food allergies. Please contact us if you have questions about a specific dietary concern.

Meeting Spaces

We have something that will fit your groups’ needs. From the main room of chapel that seats 200, to our Dining Hall that can fit 300+ guests.

Individual Facilities Rentals

Here at Wesley Woods we have a wide range of different facilities that you can rent out for any number of reasons! You can stay for a weekend or for the week!

Want a weekend away with the family and go RV'ing? We have just the place for you! 

Campsites

We have 6 sites. $25 a night. Full hookup water, electric and sewer. Common fire ring within the camper area not at each site. Must have your own shower and bathroom.

Email info@wesleywoods.com to check avialibity. Open during our shoulder seasons of summer camp: April and May or some of August, September and October.

Missionary 1, 2, and 3

Sleeps 32 People- No Kitchen (per unit)

These cabins have a central lobby that has couches and chairs and can be used as a small meeting space. Off the main lobby there are 2 sleeping sides. Each side has 8 bunk beds and can sleep 16 people. Each sleeping side also has its own bathroom, with one handicapped accessible shower, sink and toilet area separate from the rest. In addition, they have 3 more showers and individual bathroom stalls. There is no kitchen in these buildings.

Wesley Cabin

Sleeps 32 people- No Kitchen

This cabin has a central lobby that has a table and chairs and can be used as a small meeting space. Off the main lobby there are 2 sleeping sides. Each side has 8 bunk beds and can sleep 16 people. Each sleeping side also has its own bathroom has 3 showers and individual bathroom stalls. There is no kitchen in this building.

Coke Roberts

Sleeps 32 people - Small Kitchen

This cabin has a central lobby with couches and chairs that cab be used as small eating space, or tables can be set up to allow for a small eating area. There is a kitchen attached to the lobby that has a full size fridge, stove, microwave, and coffee pot. Off the center lobby this cabin has a unique set up. Instead of being a large room like our other cabins this cabin has 4 smaller rooms that have 4 bunk beds in each room. There is outside access to the rooms from the front porch. Each room has a bathroom attached to it with 2 showers, sinks and bathroom stalls.

Asbury Lodge

Sleeps 12 people - Small Kitchen

Asbury is a small cabin that is located near our pool. It has a small kitchen with a full size fridge, a small stove, coffee pot and microwave. This cabin has 2 full bathrooms one with stand up shower and the other with a shower/tub combo. There is a small living room area as well. There are 2 bedrooms each has 3 bunk beds sleeping 6 per room. There is a small front porch.

Retreat House

Sleeps 14 people - Full kitchen

This is one of our newest houses. It has an open concept kitchen, living and dining room area. The kitchen has a full size Fridge, stove, microwave, and coffee pot. There is a large dining room table and a kitchen Island that offers seating as well. This allows for the group to be within the same area and fellowship together. It is perfect for a small retreat. There are 2 smaller bedrooms that each have 2 bunk beds. There is a large bedroom with 3 bunk beds, walk in closet and an attached bathroom with a tub/shower combo. There is a common bathroom that also has a tub/shower combo.

Dining Hall

This is a great meeting space that has a projector and screen at one end of the dining hall, and a fireplace at the other. This can be set up with a large amount of round tables that can seat 6 to 8 people or with smaller groups we can use rectangular tables as well. You can use this to have a meeting with chairs in rows as well facing the projector screen. The dining hall has a men and women’s restroom by the front entrance. Wesley Woods allows use of the kitchen as long as the person in your group running the kitchen holds their serve safe certification. Otherwise we are more than happy to make meals for you.


Chapel

Our chapel has a men and women’s restroom right inside the door. There is a small room at the back that can be used for prayer or a small gathering. The upstairs of the Chapel has padded chairs that can be set up in rows and there is a projector and screen to use as well as a full sounds system. The chapel has windows lining all 3 sides of the chapel to allow the sunlight to come in. The chapel basement is an open area with tile floor. This can be set up with tables and chairs as needed for groups or to use for different activities with the open floor space.

RV Sites

6 sites, full water, sewer and electric hookups. Contact the office for reservations. Campsites open April to the end of May as well as September to the end of October. No restroom or shower facilities available.

Epworth Village Rooms

Private Rooms - Sleeps 2-4 depending on room choice - No kitchen

This is an area of camp where a group of rooms are near each other but offer individuals their privacy as well. Rooms have a private bathroom and different sleeping accommodations as described below.

  • Room 1- 1 Double bed, One Twin bed - Sleeps 3

  • Room 2- 1 Double bed, One Twin bed - Sleeps 3

  • Room 3- 1 Double bed, One Twin bed - Sleeps 3

  • Room 4- 1 Double bed, One Twin bed - Sleeps 3

  • Room 5- 2 Double beds - Sleeps 4

  • Room 6- 2 Double beds - sleeps 4

  • Room 7- 1 Double bed - Sleeps 2

  • Room 8- 1 Double bed, One Twin bed - Sleeps 3

  • Room 9 and 10 are connected with a shared bathroom there is a total of 1 double bed and 2 twins with the 2 rooms combined
