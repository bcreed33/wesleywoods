Campground Retreats & Rentals

Host Your Own Retreat

Here at Wesley Woods we love hosting groups. Our accommodations, activities and meeting spaces are perfect for any group size. So whether you’re a family reunion, work retreat, youth group, or even a church group looking for a get-away we’ve got you covered.
‍

Lodging

We have lodging for groups from 4 to 200+. All of our lodges are 4-season lodges with heat, hot water, and in-lodge toilet and bathing facilities.

Food Service

Wesley Woods happily serves most dietary restrictions such as gluten free, vegetarian, vegan, lactose intolerance, and most food allergies. Please contact us if you have questions about a specific dietary concern

Meeting Spaces

We have something that will fit your groups’ needs. From the main room of Chaple that seats 200, to our Dining Hall that can fit 300+ guest.

Checkout the Wesley Woods Hosted Events!

Wesley Woods has a handful of events that they put on every year, from men & woman's retreats to golf scrambles.

Individual Facilities Rentals

Here at Wesley Woods we have a wide range of different facilities that you can rent out for any number of reasons! You can stay for a weekend or for the week!

Want to plan a hunting trip? We have nice dry and heated houses & cabins!
Want a weekend away with the family and go RV'ing? We have just the place for you! 
‍

RV Sites

RV sites full hookup water, electric and sewage. Cost $25 a night- no onsite restroom facilities provided

Rent a House

Rent a house for a personal retreat:  weekend or week long rentals available call for pricing

Rent A Cabin

Rent a cabin sleeps 32 people in bunk beds. One cabin with a kitchen. Cabins open April to Mid June and Mid Aug to the end of Nov

Motel Units

Motel units: individual rooms with private bathrooms either 2 double beds, a double bed and a twin or 2 twin beds available for weekend or week long rentals. No kitchen access provided
