Adult Camp Weekend

July 15-17, 2022‍

Cost: $125

Ages 18+

Do you miss your time at Wesley Woods? We have the perfect experience for you. Join us for an over 18 adult weekend called Reliving Camp. This weekend you can experience all the things you enjoyed as a camper or just spend some time in the peacefulness of Wesley Woods. Gather your friends and join us Friday night starting 7pm with registration until Sunday morning at 11am. We will have worship as well as camp many different camp experiences.

**This weekend event does not qualify for the discounts offered for our week long events**
