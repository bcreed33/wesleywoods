ABC – 1st Half
Do you have a young child that is not ready to come to camp on their own, but you want them to experience Wesley Woods? ABC Camp is a half week camp where the child ages 4-6 brings an adult along with them. You will stay at camp Sunday afternoon until Tuesday afternoon. This camp follows a schedule that’s created by the lead volunteer to include crafts, pool time, a hayride, bible study, and more!

ABC – 2nd Half
Do you have a young child that is not ready to come to camp on their own, but you want them to experience Wesley Woods? ABC Camp is a half week camp where the child ages 4-6 brings an adult along with them. You will stay at camp Wednesday afternoon to Friday afternoon. This camp follows a schedule that’s created by the lead volunteer to include crafts, pool time, a hayride, bible study, and more!

ABC Adult 18+

Adult cost is included in the camper fee.

Each ABC camper is required to come with an adult over 18, for their half week of camp. Please register your child and pay the tuition and then register the adult separately. There will not be a deposit fee required for the adult, since it is paid under the child’s registration.
