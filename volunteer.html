Volunteer

Do you enjoy being outdoors mowing grass, weed eating, using a chainsaw, walking trails or stacking wood? Do you like to help cook meals or do some light cleaning? Do you like to make a project your own? If you answered yes to any of those questions Wesley Woods has a place to use your skills. We have 600 acres which means lots of outdoor work to be down or relaxing on a mower. We serve food in the summer and in the off season to groups and many hands make light work. We have projects that need completed like painting, flower planting or landscaping or other ways to beautify camp.

We can use your abilities to help continue our ministry here at Wesley Woods. Contact info@wesleywoods.com or the camp office at 814-436-7802 to sign up to come today.

Welsey Woods Work Week

Work Week is a great way to help us complete the projects that are waiting on that to do list. Join us May 14th to May 22nd. Let us know how many will be in your group and any skills you have. We have plenty of projects for all ages and abilities. Bring your group for a day or two or come and stay for the week. We have RV sites open or cabins too.
