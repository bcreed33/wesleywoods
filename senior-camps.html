Airsoft Camp

June 25 - 30
Camp Dean: Aaron Kalkbrenner
Cost: $450

Our ever-popular Airsoft Camp fills up fast! Campers learn to work together as they play different games of Airsoft. The camaraderie formed between campers in Airsoft Camp forms the perfect environment for them to feel comfortable talking about Jesus and the things that are happening in their lives. Bring your own Airsoft equipment or borrow from the camp! This camp is for our Senior campers ages 12-17.

Performing Arts Camp (PAC)

July 9 - 14
Camp Dean: Kevin & Vicki Forringer
Cost: $450

This camp has replaced our separate MACD and SACK camps. This camp focuses on our Senior campers ages 12-17 and helping them learn some more advanced skills connected with the different aspects of performing arts, if you are new to this they will make sure you get basics too!  This is great for those that want to get a behind the scenes look or take center stage all while learning about Jesus! This camp is great for those that have a passion for performance. Different sessions will be offered to help you find something of interest to you as you learn about everything that goes into making a performance happen.

Astronomy Rocketry & Computers (ARC)

July 9 - 14
Camp Dean: Stephen & Cassie Shunk
Cost: $450

Did you know that Wesley Woods has its own telescope on the property? Is your child interested in learning about astronomy as well as rockets and a bit of computers too? Campers will have the opportunity to use the telescope on camp property several times throughout the week as they learn about the vastness of God’s creation! With almost no light pollution, Wesley Woods is one of the best places to learn about the stars! Campers will also work on their own model rockets that they’ll have the chance to send into the sky at the end of the week! This camp is for our Senior campers ages 12-17.

This camp will be housed at Green Hills please pack accordingly.

July Best of Summer Sampler Sr

July 16 - 21
Camp Dean: Samantha Smith
Cost: $450

Not sure which camp you would enjoy? Want to decide some of the activities you will be doing each day? Then this is the week to come to Wesley Woods. This week of camp is different than our other weeks. Campers will be given daily options of activities to sign up for throughout the week. They can decide if they want to do fine arts, zipline, canoe, play sports, rock climb, build with legos and many more things. Evening activities will be set by our staff but the daytime will change based on your campers preferences and space available. Bible study and worship will be part of the daily routine too. Check out this new camp, it may be the perfect fit for you. This week focuses on our Senior campers ages 13-17 where they will work on learning the basics as well as some of the more advanced skills of these daily activities.
