2021 Summer Staff

*We are hiring those 18 and older at this time. We have increased the wages and food and housing is included

*Staff training starts June 5th and runs for 2 weeks and you will work between 8 and 10 weeks this summer

*You will need to have completed your mandated reporter training and have copies of your child abuse, criminal history and FBI fingerprinting completed before starting work. (volunteer clearances are not accepted for employees)

* All staff will be hired as “summer staff” unless in a special position *Summer staff members will be trained in the kitchen, crafts, counseling, adventure and lifeguarding. You will be assigned a specific job for the week but your assignment may change weekly as needed to dur camp numbers
