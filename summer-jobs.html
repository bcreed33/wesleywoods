Summer Jobs

Are you too old to be a camper? Do you have a passion for sharing your faith with others? Do you want to be part of am amazing team in the summer of 2021?  If so then please consider applying to be on our summer staff this year.Once you click apply now, create an account and it will have you complete the application and list your references with their email and contact information. Thanks for applying to join our staff!

2021 Summer Staff

*We are hiring those 18 and older at this time. We have increased the wages and food and housing is included

*Staff training starts June 5th and runs for 2 weeks and you will work between 8 and 10 weeks this summer

*You will need to have completed your mandated reporter training and have copies of your child abuse, criminal history and FBI fingerprinting (if you have lived outside of PA in the last 10 years) completed before starting work. (volunteer clearances are not accepted for employees) Clearances are only good for 5 years or until you change your address.

* All staff will be hired as “summer staff” unless in a special position members will be trained in the kitchen, crafts, counseling, adventure and lifeguarding. You will be assigned a specific job for the week but your assignment may change weekly as needed to due camp numbers
