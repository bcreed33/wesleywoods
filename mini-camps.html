Mini - A

June 27 - June 29
Camp Dean : Megan Kennedy

Mini Camp is designed for our youngest solo campers age 6-8, who aren’t quite ready for a week on their own yet. Mini Campers stay at camp from Sunday afternoon until Tuesday night. It’s the perfect amount of time to learn what camp is like for next year! Mini Campers play games, do Bible studies, crafts and more!

Mini - B

June 30 - July 2
Camp Dean : Megan Kennedy

Mini Camp is designed for our youngest solo campers age 6-8, who aren’t quite ready for a week on their own yet. Mini Campers stay at camp from Wednesday afternoon to Friday night. It’s the perfect amount of time to learn what camp is like for next year! Mini Campers play games, do Bible studies, crafts and more!

Sampler Camp

June 30 - July 2
Camp Dean : Beth McClymonds

Is your child 6 -8 and ready for a full week on their own and wants to try a little bit of everything? Then Elementary Sampler camp is the place for you! Sampler campers get to try some adventure, sports, art and specialty activities! From swimming and gaga ball to the challenge course and arts and crafts, Sampler camp has something for everyone!
